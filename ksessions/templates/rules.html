📋 Participation Rules

🏢

Sessions can be attended both online and offline, whether you are a speaker or not.

💻

Please do not bring laptops to the speaker venue — full focus on the session! 😊

🎤

Speakers can present online or offline.

📅

Applications must be submitted at least 3 calendar days before the session date.

⚡

Priority is first come, first served based on application submission time.

⚠️

Attendance for your scheduled session is mandatory. No-shows will lose first-come/first-served priority for future applications.

🗳️ Voting Criteria

Voting is done anonymously via Microsoft Forms. Each criterion is rated on a 1 to 5 scale.

1

How applicable is the topic to your day-to-day life, work or personal learning?

2

How well did the speaker involve the audience? (Workshop style, interaction, questions, activities, etc.)

3

How strong were the speaker's presentation skills? (Clarity, structure, slide quality, delivery, public speaking, body language)

4

How new, creative, or forward-thinking was the topic?

5

How original or distinctive was the information provided, beyond what is typically seen?

6

How well did you understand the topic?

✍️

Any specific written feedback to speaker(s) — not counted in the voting leaderboard.
