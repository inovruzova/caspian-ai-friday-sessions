Got Questions? 🤔

Do I need to be an expert on my topic?

Absolutely not. Imposter syndrome is welcome — just bring curiosity and a willingness to share. Your colleagues are here to learn, not to audit your LinkedIn credentials.

How long is a session?

About 60 minutes. Enough to share something meaningful, not so long that people start eyeing the exit.

What if I can't present in person?

Online is perfectly fine. Some of history's greatest presentations have been delivered in pajamas. We will never know.

Will people vote on me? That sounds terrifying.

They will — but anonymously and constructively. Think of it less as "judgement day" and more as "structured appreciation". The criteria reward effort, not perfection.

Can I present with a colleague?

Yes! Co-speakers are welcome. Just note that the gift card prize goes to the team as a whole. Decide among yourselves who deserves the bigger slice of the 200 AZN.
